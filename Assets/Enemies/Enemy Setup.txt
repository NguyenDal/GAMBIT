To setup an enemy in the level:
Turtle and Slime enemies:
1. Move the provided way points from A-D in order to the desired shape to create a loop
2. The enemy will move from D back to A when finished, creating a loop

Shooter:

1. Add the "Paricipant" to the the "Player" input on the Pea Shooter script. 
2. Position it with enough area
